## Early Hints

Nginx [doesn't seem to support 103 Early Hints yet](https://github.com/vikejs/vike/issues/1452#issuecomment-1925315908).

For example, if you use Vike's <Link href="/preloading#early-hints">`pageContext.httpResponse.earlyHints`</Link> with Node.js's [`res.writeEarlyHints()`](https://nodejs.org/api/http.html#responsewriteearlyhintshints-callback), then you'll get a `ERR_HTTP2_PROTOCOL_ERROR` error in the browser.
