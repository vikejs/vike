The gift programs ensure everyone can use Vike.

There are two programs:
- Free forever

## Who's eligible?

Your team is eligible if it has limited resources and purchasing a license key would be too expensive.

> A good rule of thumb is the number of employees. For example, we consider an organization eligible if it has **fewer than 10 paid employees**. The threshold is **more lenient in countries with lower revenue**.

If you expect more resources in the future, apply for an extended free trial. Otherwise, you can apply to use Vike for free forever.

> Eligible examples:
> - Startup
> - Open Source project
> - Amateur sports club

You can also apply for an extended free trial for other reasons (e.g. pending clearance from your company's finance department).
