Use case examples:

 - [Burda](https://www.burda.com) (one of Germany's largest media companies), migrating their diverse stacks spanning across many teams into one unified <Link href="/why#build-your-own-framework">company internal framework</Link> based on Vike and Solid.
 - [Alignable](https://www.alignable.com) (social network), using a bespoke integration of {'Ruby on Rails <=> Vike <=> React'}.
 - [Contra](https://contra.com) (freelance market), large codebase (>150 routes) with a bespoke integration of {'React <=> Vike <=> GraphQL Relay'}, and with a custom rendering strategy mixing SPA and SSR.
 - [Ecosia](https://www.ecosia.org) (search engine), migrating all their frontend from Nuxt to Vike for much better performance, and for progressively migrating from Vue 2 to Vue 3.
