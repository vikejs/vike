The free program ensures everyone can use Vike.

There are two programs:
- Free forever
- Extended free trial


## Who's eligible?

Your team is eligible if it has limited financial resources: purchasing a license would be too expensive.

> We consider an organization eligible if it has **fewer than 10 employees**, while **countries with lower income can have more employees**.

If you expect more resources in the future, apply for an extended free trial.

> Eligible examples:
> - Startup
> - Open Source (without much revenue)
> - Amateur sports club

You can also apply for an extended free trial for other reasons (e.g. pending clearance from your company's finance department).
